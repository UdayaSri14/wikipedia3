ALLU ARJUN

Allu Arjun(born 8 April 1982) is an Indian actor who works in Telugu cinema. One of the highest actors in Indian cinema, Allu Arjun has been featured in Forbes Indian's Celebrity 100 list since 2014.He is a recepient of several awards including a National Film Awards, six Filmfare awards, and three Nandi Awards. He is considered to be one of the finest dancers in Indian cinema. He is popularly referred yo as "Stylish Star" and "Icon Star".Allu Arjun made his debut with Gangotri in 2003. He rose to prominence starring in Sukumars's cult classic Arya(2004)for which he earned a Nandi Special Jury Award. He consolidated his reputation with tha ction films Bunny(2005)and Desamuduru(2007).In 2008,he started in the romantic drama Parugu for which he won his first Filmfare Award for Best Actor - Telugu.
